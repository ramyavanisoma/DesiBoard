Credit Score Information

Your credit score is a key factor in your financial health, influencing your ability to obtain credit and the terms of loans.

Key Components:

  • Payment History: Your track record of paying bills on time is the most significant factor in your score. Check your score at MyFICO.
  • Credit Utilization: The ratio of your current credit card balances to your credit limits. More information can be found on TransUnion - Credit Utilization.
  • Length of Credit History: The age of your credit accounts affects your score. Older accounts are generally better. Read more on Credit Karma - Length of Credit History.
  • Types of Credit: A mix of credit types (e.g., credit cards, loans) can positively impact your score. Learn more at Experian - Types of Credit.
  • New Credit: Applying for new credit frequently can impact your score negatively. More details are available at FICO - Understanding Your Credit Score.
