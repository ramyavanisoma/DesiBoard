Child and Education Services

  • Education Rights:
    • All children in the U.S. have the right to public education regardless of their immigration status. Contact local school districts for enrollment information and support.
  • Child Welfare and Advocacy:
    • Child Welfare Information Gateway (CWIG)
    • Kids in Need of Defense (KIND)

Community and Cultural Support

  • Indian Cultural Organizations:
    • India Association of Greater Boston (IAGB)
    • Bay Area Indian Community
  • Religious and Cultural Centers:
    • Temples, gurdwaras, mosques, and churches often provide community support, counseling, and emergency assistance.

Important Tips

  • Stay Informed: Keep up to date with local laws and policies regarding immigration.
  • Seek Trusted Help: Use reputable organizations and legal services to avoid scams or misinformation.
  • Document Everything: Keep records of all interactions with authorities, legal documents, and correspondence.
